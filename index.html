About Us

Azam Afridi Goods Transport Company traces its roots back to 1920, when the father of Mr. Sharaf Gul Afridi began his journey in Pakistan’s early transport industry. After years of dedication, Mr. Sharaf Gul Afridi established AAGTC in 1984 — named after his son Azam Afridi. Today, with over 100 years of family legacy, AAGTC proudly operates 300+ vehicles across Pakistan, serving major corporations with honesty, reliability, and commitment.
