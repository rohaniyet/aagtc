About Us

A Century of Trust in Transportation The legacy of Azam Afridi Goods Transport Company traces back to 1920, when the father of Mr. Sharaf Gul Afridi began his journey in Pakistan’s early transport industry. Young Sharaf Gul Afridi closely observed and assisted his father, gaining valuable experience and a lifelong passion for logistics and transportation. After years of dedication and hands-on learning, Mr. Sharaf Gul Afridi founded Azam Afridi Goods Transport Company in 1984, naming it after his son Azam Afridi — symbolizing a proud family legacy built on trust, hard work, and continuity. With over 100 years of combined experience and a fleet of 300+ vehicles, including trucks, containers, and vans, the company has become one of Pakistan’s most reliable names in logistics. For decades, Azam Afridi Transport has proudly served some of Multinational & Pakistan’s largest corporations, offering safe, timely, and dependable delivery services across the nation. Our foundation rests on three enduring values: Honesty, Reliability, and Commitment. Our mission is clear — to deliver every shipment safely, swiftly, and professionally, every single time.
